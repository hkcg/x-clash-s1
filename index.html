A New World Awaits

一個全新的世界等待著您

The transition into Season 1 brings a massive visual and structural overhaul to the X-Clash map. Unlike the pre-season normal map which featured a standard layout, Season 1 introduces a highly structured, grid-based terrain system. This new design not only looks cleaner but fundamentally changes how alliances plan their expansions and how players interact with the environment.

過渡到第一賽季為超能戰爭地圖帶來了巨大的視覺和結構改造。與採用標準佈局的季前賽普通地圖不同，第一賽季引入了高度結構化的網格地形系統。這種新設計不僅看起來更簡潔，而且從根本上改變了聯盟規劃擴張以及玩家與環境互動的方式。

Introducing: The New Square Levels

隆重介紹：全新的方形關卡

One of the most noticeable differences in Season 1 is the introduction of the new square levels. These distinct, perfectly squared resource and challenge nodes replace the organic, scattered nodes of the pre-season map. This makes identifying high-value targets and organizing alliance territory much more strategic.

第一賽季最顯著的變化之一是引入了全新的方形關卡。這些獨特、完美的方形資源和挑戰節點取代了季前賽地圖中有機、分散的節點。這使得識別高價值目標和組織聯盟領地變得更具戰略性。

Level Progression Expanded: The pre-season map had a limited tier of nodes. Season 1 pushes the boundaries by adding 1 more Level 6 node type to the mid-game progression, bridging the gap to the ultimate end-game nodes like the massive Level 10 squares!
關卡進度擴展： 季前賽地圖的節點層級有限。第一賽季突破了界限，在中期進度中增加了1 個額外的 6 級節點類型，彌合了通往巨大 10 級方形等最終遊戲節點的差距！

Exploring the Lower Tiers

探索較低層級

It's not just the top-end that got a revamp. The early to mid-game progression has been smoothed out with beautifully redesigned square tiles for the lower levels. The addition of the new Level 6 nodes ensures that players always have a steady stepping stone as they grow their power.

不僅僅是頂級節點得到了改造。早期到中期的進度也變得更加平滑，為較低級別重新設計了精美的方形圖塊。全新 6 級節點的加入確保了玩家在增強實力時始終有穩定的墊腳石。

Macro Strategy & Map Navigation

宏觀戰略與地圖導航

Because the Season 1 map utilizes these new square levels, zooming out gives commanders a much clearer tactical view of the battlefield. Moving around the map feels more deliberate, as every square tile represents a specific challenge, resource, or strategic chokepoint.

因為第一賽季地圖使用了這些新的方形關卡，縮小視角能為指揮官提供更清晰的戰場戰術視圖。在地圖上移動感覺更加深思熟慮，因為每個方形圖塊都代表著特定的挑戰、資源或戰略咽喉。
